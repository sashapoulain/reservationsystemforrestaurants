Frequently asked questions

How does the app determine my current location for finding nearby restaurants?
The app utilizes your device's GPS functionality to determine your precise location. It ensures accurate results by accessing location data, allowing you to discover restaurants in close proximity.
Can I make reservations directly through the app, and how does this process work?
Yes, you can make reservations within the app. Simply select the desired restaurant, check for availability, and choose your preferred time. The app will then handle the reservation process, providing you with a confirmation and ensuring a hassle-free dining experience.
